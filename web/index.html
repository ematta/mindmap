About MindMap

MindMap is a lightweight, client-side brainstorming tool. Create boards, add ideas as draggable cards, and connect them to visualize your thoughts.

Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is sent to or stored on any server — your boards, notes, and connections are saved only in your browser's local storage.
